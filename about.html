How to get started with Quick Springs

  1. Apply Now! or give us a call at 601-507-7250 to get started
  2. Get pre-approved for a 24-Month Lease
  3. Schedule an estimate with one of our expert installers
  4. Review our Terms and Conditions and complete our 24-Month Lease Agreement
  5. Schedule your installation with one of our expert installers and enjoy the benefits of a new High Efficiency Water Heater

How the 24 Month Lease works

  1. You choose a High Efficiency Water Heater that meets your needs with the help of one of our expert installers
  2. We work with you to find the best lease option given your financial needs
  3. You complete a 24-Month Lease Agreement with one of our Representatives by calling 601-507-7250
  4. Your affordable, worry-free lease covers maintenance, repairs, and we will replace your High Efficiency Water Heater with a similar model when it is time for replacement
  5. After your lease ends, you can either buy the water heater at a discount or continue your lease on a month to month basis at a reduced rate
